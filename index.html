Our Story

Happy Belly is the first eco virtual resturant in Lagos Nigeria, serving on-demand and customizable gourmet meals founded address limited access to nutritious,high quality meals,over dependence on imported ingredients , environmental harm and food wastage.

We have pioneered a substainable food system, sourcing 100% organic produce locally, adopting eco-friendly practices, and educating our community to minimize environmental impact.

We believe the future of food is substainable let's shape it together one eco-conscious meal at a time!

Our Purpose

We are on a journey to change the way you experience food.

We believe in the power of food to bring people together, to inspire joy and gratitude,and a shared commitment to our well-being and the planet's.
